Our story

Founded on the belief that care should feel personal

Hands Over Health Care LLC was born from a passion for serving others. Founder and CEO Brianna Courtland saw too many families struggling to find in-home care that was both highly skilled and genuinely caring — care that treated their loved ones as people, not tasks.

So she built something different: a traveling caregiver agency rooted in compassion, where experienced CNAs deliver dependable support and families stay in control of who provides it. Today, Hands Over Health Care proudly serves Wayne County and the Greater Detroit area, helping seniors and individuals live safely and comfortably in the place they love most — home.
